# Brand & Content

> Define your personal brand so the agent produces aligned content — and help you show up consistently across channels.

**Status:** Not activated

To activate this module, run `/onboard` and select "Brand & Content", or follow the manual setup guide in the docs.

---

## What Gets Set Up

- Brand strategy document defining your positioning, audience, voice, and differentiation
- Voice guide with tone spectrum, language rules, and examples of on-brand vs. off-brand writing
- Content queue for drafting, staging, and scheduling posts across channels
- Brand Strategist agent trained on your voice and content system
- `/content` command for on-demand content drafting, scoring, and calendar management
- Engagement coaching during daily operations — reply suggestions, thread hooks, repurposing opportunities flagged

---

## What Your Agent Does For You

**Voice enforcement on drafts:**
> "This draft is a bit passive for your brand. You tend to lead with the contrarian take, not the qualifier. Compare: 'It's worth considering that most productivity advice...' vs. 'Most productivity advice is designed for average performers. You're not average.' The second one is more you. Want me to rewrite it in your voice?"

**STEPPS content scoring before you post:**
> "Scoring this draft: Social Currency (8/10 — contrarian take earns status), Triggers (4/10 — no environmental hook), Emotion (7/10 — mild frustration trigger), Public (6/10 — visible behavior, could be stronger), Practical Value (9/10 — immediately actionable), Stories (5/10 — no narrative arc). Strongest levers to improve: add a trigger reference and a one-sentence story."

**Content calendar management:**
> "Your content queue has 4 posts ready. Based on your last 30 days of posting, Tuesday and Thursday mornings get 2-3x your average engagement. I'd suggest scheduling this one for Tuesday 7am CT. Want me to queue it?"

**Process-sharing suggestions from your actual work:**
> "You just spent 90 minutes designing that decision framework. That's a content brick — your audience would find the process valuable. A thread on 'how I structured this decision' would perform well. I can draft a thread outline from your notes if you want."

## Source Materials

The Brand Strategist agent draws principles from:

- *Building a StoryBrand* — Donald Miller (clarifying your message, the hero framework, making the customer the hero)
- *Contagious* — Jonah Berger (STEPPS framework — Social Currency, Triggers, Emotion, Public, Practical Value, Stories)
- *Show Your Work!* — Austin Kleon (sharing the process, building in public, finding your audience through generosity)
