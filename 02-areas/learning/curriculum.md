## Knowledge Domains

Map your knowledge across the areas that matter to you. Rate honestly — this helps the agent route study time effectively.

| Domain | Current Level (1-5) | Target Level | Priority | Notes |
|--------|-------------------|-------------|----------|-------|
| [e.g., Computer Science] | 4 | 5 | Active | Professional experience |
| [e.g., Finance] | 2 | 3 | Next | Need for personal goals |
| [e.g., Biology] | 1 | 2 | Someday | Curious but not urgent |

**Level guide:**
1. Awareness — you've heard of the key concepts
2. Literacy — you can have an informed conversation
3. Competence — you can apply the knowledge practically
4. Proficiency — you can teach others and handle complex problems
5. Mastery — deep expertise, able to create new knowledge in the field
