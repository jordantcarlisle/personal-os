# Financial Organization — Onboarding

> All questions are optional — share only what you're comfortable with.

## Questions

1. **Are you interested in having your agent help with finances?**
   (This module is entirely opt-in.)

2. **What financial areas would be most useful?**
   - Budget tracking and spending awareness
   - Savings goals and progress
   - Investment tracking (not advice — just tracking)
   - Debt payoff planning
   - Tax preparation support
   - Business/freelance finances
   - Major purchase decisions
   - Financial review prep (quarterly)

3. **Do you currently use any financial tools?**
   (Mint, YNAB, spreadsheet, bank app, accountant)

4. **What's your primary financial goal right now?**

5. **Do you have a budget or spending plan?**
   - Yes, and I follow it
   - Yes, but I don't follow it well
   - No — the agent can help create one using the Conscious Spending Plan framework

6. **What's your target savings/investment rate?**
   (% of income — this becomes the "wealth-building rate" the agent tracks)

7. **Any upcoming large financial events?**
   (Tax deadlines, large purchases, contract renewals, life changes)

## How Your Answers Are Used

- Questions 2-3 → `02-areas/finances/financial-overview.md`
- Questions 4-6 → Financial Advisor agent configuration
- Financial data is the most sensitive — stays strictly local
- The agent tracks and analyzes but NEVER prescribes specific investments
