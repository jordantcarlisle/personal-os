# Brand & Content — Onboarding

> These questions help your Brand Strategist agent produce content that sounds like you.

## Questions

1. **Would you like to define a personal brand to help the agent produce aligned content?**

2. **What platforms are you active on?**
   (X/Twitter, LinkedIn, Instagram, YouTube, TikTok, Blog/Newsletter, Podcast)

3. **Who is your target audience?**
   (Be specific: demographics, interests, problems they have)

4. **What topics do you want to be known for?**
   (Your 3-5 content pillars)

5. **Describe your voice in 3-5 adjectives.**
   (Examples: warm, direct, contrarian, evidence-based, playful, authoritative)

6. **Who do you admire in your space?**
   (2-3 people whose content style you respect — helps calibrate)

7. **What should your content NEVER sound like?**
   (Examples: corporate, preachy, clickbaity, generic)

8. **What's your content goal?**
   - Build an audience for a future product/service
   - Establish thought leadership
   - Document my journey and share learnings
   - Grow a community around shared interests

9. **How often do you want to post?**
   (Consistency matters more than frequency)

10. **Do you have existing content or a backlog of ideas?**

## How Your Answers Are Used

- Questions 2-4 → `02-areas/brand/brand-strategy.md`
- Questions 5-7 → `02-areas/brand/voice-guide.md`
- Questions 8-10 → `02-areas/brand/content-queue.md`
- Your voice guide becomes the standard every draft is checked against
