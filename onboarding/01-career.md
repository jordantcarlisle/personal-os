# Career & Role Management — Onboarding

> These questions help your Career Coach agent understand your professional context.

## Questions

1. **What's your current role and title?**

2. **What does your organization do?** (Brief — one sentence is fine)

3. **Who do you report to?** (Name, title, what they care about)

4. **Who reports to you?** (Direct reports — names and roles, or "none" if IC)

5. **What are your key responsibilities?** (The 3-5 things you're actually evaluated on)

6. **What does success look like in your role this quarter?** (Specific outcomes, not activities)

7. **Do you have regular 1:1s?** (With whom, how often)

8. **Do you have recurring team rituals?** (Standups, retros, all-hands — list them)

9. **Who are your key stakeholders?** (People outside your direct team who care about your work)
   For each: name, role, what they care about, how often they need updates

10. **What's the biggest challenge in your role right now?**

11. **What skills are you trying to develop professionally?** (This connects to your learning curriculum)

12. **Is there anything politically sensitive about your work situation?** (Optional — helps the agent navigate carefully)

## How Your Answers Are Used

- Questions 1-5 → `02-areas/career/role-definition.md`
- Questions 4, 7-8 → `02-areas/career/team-map.md`
- Question 9 → `02-areas/career/stakeholder-registry.md`
- Questions 6, 10-11 → Career Coach agent context
- All answers stay local in your repo
