# Health Data Integration — Onboarding

> These questions help your Health Analyst agent understand your health context and goals.

## Questions

1. **What wearables or health devices do you use?**
   (Examples: Oura Ring, Apple Watch, Whoop, Garmin, Fitbit, Eight Sleep, CGM, smart scale)

2. **What health data do you currently track?** (Check all that apply)
   - [ ] Sleep (duration, quality)
   - [ ] Heart rate / HRV
   - [ ] Steps / Activity
   - [ ] Workouts / Exercise
   - [ ] Nutrition / Diet
   - [ ] Weight / Body composition
   - [ ] Blood glucose (CGM)
   - [ ] Blood work / Lab results
   - [ ] Meditation / Mindfulness
   - [ ] Other: ___

3. **What are your top 3 health goals right now?**
   (Examples: sleep 7+ hours, lose 15 lbs, run a 5K, reduce stress, build muscle)

4. **Do you follow any specific protocols or frameworks?**
   (Examples: intermittent fasting, specific diet, training program, supplement stack)

5. **Are you running any health experiments?**
   (Something you're testing with a defined protocol and timeline)

6. **What does your current exercise routine look like?**
   (Type, frequency, duration — or "none currently")

7. **Any health conditions or limitations the agent should know about?**
   (Optional — helps avoid inappropriate recommendations)

8. **Would you like daily health briefings during your morning reflection?**
   (Recommended — this is where the most value comes from)

9. **How much detail do you want?**
   - [ ] Just the highlights (1-2 sentences)
   - [ ] Moderate detail (key metrics + one recommendation)
   - [ ] Full analysis (all available data with interpretation)

## How Your Answers Are Used

- Questions 1-2 → `02-areas/health-data/integration-status.md`
- Questions 3-6 → `02-areas/health-data/wellness-framework.md`
- Questions 7-9 → Health Analyst agent configuration
- All data stays local — health information never leaves your system
