# Relationship Management — Onboarding

> These questions help your Relationship Manager agent understand how you want to maintain connections.

## Questions

1. **How do you currently keep track of important relationships?**
   (CRM, spreadsheet, notes app, memory, nothing)

2. **What categories of relationships matter most to you?**
   - Mentors / Advisors
   - Professional peers / Colleagues
   - Direct reports / Team members
   - Friends
   - Family
   - Industry contacts / Network
   - Accountability partners

3. **List 5-10 of your most important relationships.**
   For each: name, relationship type, how often you'd ideally connect

4. **How often do you want to be nudged to reach out to people?**
   - Daily (surface 1-2 people)
   - Weekly (during review)
   - Only when someone is significantly overdue

5. **Do you have regular 1:1s or recurring meetings with specific people?**

6. **What's your preferred outreach style?**
   (Quick text, email, phone call, in-person, varies by relationship)

7. **Is there anyone you've lost touch with that you'd like to reconnect with?**

8. **Any relationships that need special care or attention right now?**

## How Your Answers Are Used

- Questions 1-3 → `02-areas/relationships/contact-framework.md`
- Questions 4-6 → Relationship Manager agent configuration
- Questions 7-8 → Immediate outreach suggestions
- Contact details stay local — never shared externally
