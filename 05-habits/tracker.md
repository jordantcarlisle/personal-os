## Recovery Protocol

Missing a habit isn't the problem. What you do next is.

1. **Notice it** — Don't pretend it didn't happen. Awareness is the first move.
2. **Don't judge** — One miss doesn't define you. It's a data point, not a verdict.
3. **Never miss twice** — Get back on track immediately. Tomorrow is the only day that matters right now.
4. **Ask why** — Was it environment, energy, or commitment? The answer tells you what to fix.
5. **Adjust if needed** — Maybe the habit needs to be smaller, earlier, or paired with something else.

> The goal isn't perfection. The goal is consistency. Missing once is an accident. Missing twice is the start of a new pattern.
