# Chief of Staff — Quick Capture

Capture an item to the inbox instantly. Speed over organization.

## Process

1. Take the user's input — whatever they just said or described
2. Append to the Capture Zone section of `00-cockpit/inbox.md`:
   ```
   - [ ] [item with enough context to understand later]
   ```
3. Confirm: "Captured to inbox."
4. Ask: "Process now or save for later?"
   - **Process now:** Walk through the GTD processing checklist in inbox.md
   - **Save for later:** Done — move on with no friction
