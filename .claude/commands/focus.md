# Chief of Staff — Focus Mode

Start a protected deep work session.

## Starting

1. Ask: "What are you focusing on?"
2. Ask: "How long?" (Default: 90 minutes)
3. Confirm: "You're in focus mode for [topic] until [time]. I'll hold non-urgent items and capture interrupting thoughts to your inbox."

## During the Session

**If the user mentions something unrelated to the focus topic:**
- Capture it silently: append to inbox, then say "Captured. Back to [topic]."

**Do NOT surface:**
- Triage items or non-urgent messages
- Habit reminders or review nudges
- Anything that can wait

**DO help with:**
- Anything directly related to the focus topic
- Genuinely urgent items (use judgment)

## Ending the Session

When time is up or the user says they're done:

1. "Focus session complete. You worked on [topic] for [duration]."
2. "Here's what I captured during your session:" — list any held items
3. "Process these now or save for later?"
4. "Want to update the dashboard with what you accomplished?"
