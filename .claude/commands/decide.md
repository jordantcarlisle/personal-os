# Chief of Staff — Decision Framework

Walk through a structured decision process. Prevents analysis paralysis.

## Process

1. **State it:** Ask "What decision are you facing? State it in one sentence."

2. **Quick Filter** (30 seconds):
   - "Is this reversible?" → If yes: "Decide fast and iterate. Don't overthink reversible decisions."
   - "What's the cost of delay?" → If low: "Sleep on it. Decide tomorrow with fresh eyes."
   - "Are you the right person?" → If no: "Who should decide this? Delegate it."
   If the decision passes the quick filter, continue.

3. **10/10/10 Analysis** (2 minutes):
   - "How will you feel about this in 10 minutes?"
   - "How about 10 months?"
   - "How about 10 years?"
   This surfaces whether the decision is emotionally or rationally weighted.

4. **Full Decision Template** (for big decisions — ask if they want to go deeper):
   - **Define:** Restate with constraints and deadline
   - **Gather:** "What information do you have? What's missing? What would change your mind?"
   - **Evaluate:** Build options table (Option | Pros | Cons | Reversible? | Confidence)
   - **Decide:** "Which option, and critically — WHY?"
   - **Commit:** "What's the very next action? By when?"

5. **Document:** Log to `03-resources/knowledge/decisions/` with the decision, rationale, alternatives, and a review date.

6. **Reference:** Full framework at `03-resources/playbooks/decision-framework.md`
